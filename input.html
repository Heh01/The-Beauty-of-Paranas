Explore The Beauty of Paranas

Everyone needs to unwind and enjoy life even just for once to forget all the worries for a bit, especially now that we're dealing with a pandemic. We all have different ways of releasing stress, whether it's through hobbies or traveling and exploring new places. As we tackle traveling and exploring, have you already heard of the place named Paranas?

Paranas Samar (formerly known as Wright) is located in Region Vlll Western Samar and is also known as the place of Phillippine Eagle because it was once the home of most of the Eagles back then.


This place is barely known by many people, yet it will astonish you with its many amazing sites to see. Falls, caves, rivers, and a variety of cold spring resorts are all sites you could enjoy. If you're looking for a challenge, try hiking in different forests and enjoy the fresh air. This location features farms and seas, so you'll be able to eat fresh fruits, vegetables, and seafood.


Because it is well-known and frequented by tourists, the Ulot River is one of the rivers in Paranas Province that every Paranasnon feels proud of. One of the activities you must experience is the Torpedo Extreme Boat Ride, which is on Samar's bucket list. Beautiful places such as Liaw Cave and Nasarang Falls are also worth seeing. Cold spring resorts like Villa Escober, SINP, and Burabod Spring are highly recommended for you if you're looking for some spots that you can afford. It's for unwinding, spending quality time with family, and commemorating with any occasion. With a small amount of money, you get to enjoy their refreshing and relaxing cold waters that won't fail you.


But did you know that it's not just the places that are the beautiful ones here in Paranas? There are also many beautiful faces, as well as kind-hearted and friendly individuals. When you explore Paranas, you meet a lot of friendly people who are eager to help and welcome you.


"Explore the world while you're young because once you get old, you'll lose the chance," people say. Explore and take advantage of the opportunity to travel the world while you still can, and if you haven't visited Paranas yet, this is a sign that you should. Its beauty and people will not disappoint you. In a world full of worries and difficulties, this place will bring you peace because of its breathtaking view of a golden sunset merging the sea with the birds chirping on green trees. So, what do you have to lose? Come see everything Paranas has to offer!
